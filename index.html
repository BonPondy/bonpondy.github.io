Intro

Bonpondy Foundation envisages to achieve the prime object of the Trust is to consecrated and render whatever possible assistance within the means of its Development Fund and the sources raised by the trust by involving people and in association with Government and other Service Organizations in the field of educational development and thereby rendering its services without profit motive and without any distinction on account of caste, creed, colour, nationality, sex orreligionto the poor, deserving and needy sector of the economy. Motto

  1. To establish an ideal educational paradigm to prepare individuals from a very tender age onwards to grow into perfect living beings by gradually unfolding successively and simultaneously their physical, vital, mental and psychic potentialities.
  2. To develop new curriculum, methodologies, teaching-learning tools and disciplines of knowledge by making a needed shift from the content-oriented learning to a new way of learning, faculty training, faculty development and intuitive knowledge and experience.
  3. To bring about an integral change for integral perfection in life individually and collectively.
  4. To help and promote education and learning including Primary, Secondary, and College Education (Technical, Non- Technical, Agriculture, Engineering, Medical, Commercial, Scientific, Computer and Information Technology, or any other subject of Education) and research work in all subjects among boys and girls in schools, colleges and other institutions to enable them to become useful citizens.
  5. To create general awareness and impart social and physical education among the people.
  6. To help in the supply of maintenance of professors and teachers thereof, on of books and other necessaries incidental to the carrying out of such education and others educational measures as may from time to time deem good and necessary in the opinion of the Trustees.
  7. To conduct tutorial classes and vocational training programmes, to promote general knowledge among all sections of the people and to coach for entrance examinations and recruitment tests.
  8. To organize, establish, carry out, maintain and develop educational institutions, provide lodging and boarding facility for the students and desire youth irrespective of religion, race, caste and languages.
  9. To start establish and promote educational or charitable institutions for the diffusion of useful knowledge, literary, scientific, technical or social and to apply for and to obtain for the whenever necessary recognition from the Government or University, Educational or other authorities as the case may be.
  10. To print, publish, edit, issue and exhibit any literature, books, journals, paper, magazines, souvenirs and all other reading materials for the diffusion of useful knowledge and for the promotion, benefit and advancement of the educational, cultural, social, charitable ideals, work facilities of the society.
  11. To acquire, take over or manage, and bring under its possession or control, any existing schools and educational institutions with all their subsisting rights and to all extent its liabilities and assets and manage the same as the owner thereof.
  12. To carry on any social service work or relief work for helping the poor, disabled or others affected by natural calamities, either by itself or in cooperation with any other organizations.
  13. To enter into arrangements, amalgamation with any other association, institutions, societies or Trusts having similar objects of this Trust;
  14. To pay stipends and scholarships to the students on such terms as the trustees may deem fit.
